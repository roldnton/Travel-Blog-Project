Learn More About Visayas!

The Visayas is the central island group of the Philippines and is made up of islands known for white-sand beaches, diving spots, colorful festivals, and heritage destinations. It includes major provinces such as Cebu, Bohol, Iloilo, Leyte, Negros, and Samar, making it a favorite for travelers who want a mix of culture, food, and coastal adventure in one region.

Boracay

Famous for powdery white sand, clear water, and sunsets that turn the shoreline gold.

Chocolate Hills

One of Bohol's most iconic natural wonders, with hundreds of rounded hills spread across the landscape.

Kalanggaman Island

A serene island destination known for its long sandbar and postcard-like sea views.

Notable Places to Visit in Visayas!

Cebu

A dynamic mix of city life, history, island hopping, waterfalls, and access to some of the country's most popular marine adventures.

Siquijor

Known for peaceful beaches, clear waters, waterfalls, and an island atmosphere filled with quiet charm and local folklore.

Iloilo

A heritage-rich city celebrated for its churches, food scene, and the world-famous Dinagyang Festival.
