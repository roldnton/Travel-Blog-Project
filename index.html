How it works

The highlighted island groups are clickable. Hover or tab to them to open a destination pop-up, then click to visit the section page for that island group.

Map mood

The page checks the current time in Asia/Manila. Daytime shows a brighter sky tint, while nighttime adds a darker overlay and star field for a map-at-night feel.

Interaction

Use the controls to zoom in, zoom out, or reset. You can also drag the map inside its frame on desktop for a more map-like browsing experience.
