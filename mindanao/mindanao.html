Notable Cities to Visit in Mindanao!

Vigan City

Established in the 16th century, Vigan is the third oldest settlement established by the Spanish Colonizers, and is the best-preserved example of a planned Spanish colonial town in Asia.

Tagaytay City

Boasts outstanding scenery and cooler climate, this city overlooks Taal Lake in Batangas, and provides a clear view of Taal Volcano Island from several vantage points in the city.

Baguio City

Dubbed as the "City of Pines," Baguio City is popular in the summer for its unusually cool weather, as it has an approximate elevation of 1,500 meters above sea level. It is also the largest city in Benguet!
