Learn More About Mindanao!

Mindanao is the second-largest island group in the Philippines and is known for its wide mountain ranges, fertile lands, beaches, lakes, and diverse communities. It offers a different side of the country through surf destinations, cool upland escapes, cultural festivals, and natural attractions that range from powerful waterfalls to peaceful island resorts.

Siargao

A global surf favorite with lagoons, palm-lined roads, and a relaxed island lifestyle.

Camiguin

A compact island packed with volcanoes, springs, white sandbars, and beautiful coastal drives.

Tinuy-an Falls

One of Mindanao's most stunning waterfalls, known for its wide curtain-like flow.

Notable Places to Visit in Mindanao!

Samal Island

A tropical escape near Davao with beach resorts, clear water, and quick island-style getaways.

Lake Sebu

Known for cool scenery, cultural experiences, and relaxing upland views that feel far from the city rush.

Dahilayan

A highland destination in Bukidnon known for cool weather, outdoor adventure, and mountain resort vibes.
