Luzon festivals

North to South

Luzon mixes flower festivals, harvest celebrations, and giant lantern traditions with a blend of heritage, food, and city energy.

  • Panagbenga – Baguio's famous flower festival known for floats, blooms, and street dancing.
  • Pahiyas – A colorful Lucban celebration where homes are decorated with harvest-inspired designs.
  • Giant Lantern Festival – Pampanga's bright holiday showcase of intricate parol craftsmanship.

Visayas festivals

Island Celebrations

The Visayas is packed with high-energy street festivals, bold costumes, drumbeats, and celebrations that draw huge crowds every year.

  • Sinulog – Cebu's iconic festival filled with dances, chants, and grand processions.
  • Dinagyang – Iloilo's performance-heavy celebration known for striking costumes and rhythmic street showcases.
  • MassKara – Bacolod's smiling-mask festival celebrated with music, dancing, and colorful parades.

Mindanao festivals

Culture and Nature

Mindanao festivals often highlight indigenous culture, harvest abundance, and the region's strong connection to music, community, and craft.

  • Kadayawan – Davao's well-known thanksgiving festival celebrating culture, produce, and community pride.
  • Lanzones Festival – Camiguin's lively island celebration inspired by its famous sweet lanzones fruit.
  • Kaamulan – Bukidnon's gathering of indigenous traditions, performances, and heritage showcases.
